About Me

Full Stack Developer

I'm a versatile developer with experience across the entire web development stack. I enjoy creating dynamic, interactive experiences that bring ideas to life, focusing on usability and performance. On the *backend*, I build robust, scalable, and secure server-side applications using technologies like [Node.js, Python, Java, Express etc.]. I thrive in environments where I can work on all aspects of a project, from designing sleek UIs to building reliable backend systems. I’m passionate about continuous learning, whether it’s exploring new frameworks, optimising code, or contributing to open-source projects. I enjoy collaborating with cross-functional teams and believe in writing clean, maintainable code to create impactful digital experiences.
